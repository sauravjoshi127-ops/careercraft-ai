📊 What are ATS Score & Relevance Score?

Every application you submit faces two filters — an algorithm and a human. These two scores predict how well your cover letter will pass both:

🤖 ATS Score (Applicant Tracking System)
Most companies use ATS software to automatically scan and filter applications before any human reads them. The ATS looks for keywords and phrases from the job description. A low ATS score means your application may never reach the hiring manager. Aim for 70 or higher.
🎯 Relevance Score
Once past the ATS, a real person reads your letter. The Relevance Score measures how closely your skills, experience, achievements, and personality match what the hiring manager actually wants. High relevance = interview invitation. Aim for 75 or higher.
